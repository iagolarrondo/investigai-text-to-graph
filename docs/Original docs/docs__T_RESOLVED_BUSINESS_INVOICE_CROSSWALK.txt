Each entry in T_RESOLVED_BUSINESS_INVOICE_CROSSWALK corresponds to a relationship between a business (identified by RES_BUSINESS_ID) and an invoice (identified by NORM_INVOICE_ID).
The EDGE_NAME column identifies the type of relationship. It can only have the following value:
- PROVIDED_CARE_ON_INVOICE: The business was the care provider for the given invoice.

Use this table to find all invoices submitted by a particular provider agency or care facility,
or to identify the business entity behind a given invoice.

For invoices submitted by individual caregivers (ICPs) rather than business entities,
use T_RESOLVED_PERSON_INVOICE_CROSSWALK instead.

Join path:
  T_NORM_INVOICE
    -> T_RESOLVED_BUSINESS_INVOICE_CROSSWALK on NORM_INVOICE_ID
    -> T_RESOLVED_BUSINESS on RES_BUSINESS_ID
